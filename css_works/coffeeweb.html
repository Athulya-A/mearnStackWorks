Our Products...

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Austria with later development taking place in Italy, and is prepared with steamed milk foam.

Irish Coffee

Irish coffee is a cocktail consisting of hot coffee, Irish whiskey, and sugar, stirred, and topped with cream.

Mocha

A café mocha, also called mocaccino, is a chocolate-flavoured warm beverage that is a variant of a café latte, commonly served in a glass rather than a mug.

Red Eye Coffee

Red eye coffee is a coffee drink that combines drip coffee with one or two shots of espresso.

Doppio

Doppio espresso is a double shot which is extracted using double the amount of ground coffee in a larger-sized portafilter basket.

Espressos

Espresso is a coffee-brewing method of Italian origin, in which a small amount of nearly boiling water is forced under 9–10 bars of pressure through finely-ground coffee beans.
